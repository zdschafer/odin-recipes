Burger

Description

Behold the epitome of burger bliss: a tantalizing creation that beckons with its golden brioche bun cradling a perfectly grilled, medium-rare patty. Adorned with a decadent melt of aged cheddar and gouda, it boasts a mouthwatering symphony of textures and flavors. Vibrant layers of crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and thinly sliced red onions add a burst of freshness, creating a visual feast. Crowned with a secret sauce, this burger is a work of art, promising a delicious journey with each savory, satisfying bite.

Ingredients

  • Beef Patty
  • Chedder Cheese
  • Gouda Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onions
  • Secret Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the patty - season generously
  2. Grill the patty - medium rare heat, ensure even cooking
  3. Prepare brioche bun - lightly toast to a golden brown
  4. Melt the cheese - just before removing the patty, top it with generous amount of chedder and gouda cheese
  5. Assemble the layers
  6. Secret Sauce - blend of aoili, barbaque, and chipotle mayo
