Eggs

Description

Picture a plate adorned with a culinary masterpiece of eggs, a visual feast that awakens the senses. Nestled against each other, the eggs showcase a flawless balance of a runny yolk and perfectly set whites. The golden yolks, like liquid sunshine, beckon with an irresistible richness. Delicately seasoned with a pinch of salt and a sprinkle of freshly ground pepper, these eggs invite you to indulge in their simple yet profound elegance. The plate is completed with a garnish of finely chopped chives, adding a touch of freshness to the composition. It's a breakfast tableau that promises not just sustenance but a delightful culinary experience, inviting you to savor the artistry of a well-prepared plate of eggs.

Ingredients

  • Carton of Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Prepare eggs - crack eggs into bowl
  2. Season - pinch of salt and ground pepper
  3. Cook
  4. Top with chives
